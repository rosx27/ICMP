How to Download and Run ICMP Installer

Download the Installer

Click the button below to download the ICMP Installer:

Download ICMP Installer

Step 1

When you see the message "ICMP Installer.exe isn't commonly downloaded. Make sure you trust ICMP Installer.exe before you open it", click on the three dots to show more options.

Step 2

Click on "Keep" from the dropdown menu.

Step 3

You'll see a warning from Microsoft Defender SmartScreen. Click the "Show more" dropdown menu.

Step 4

Click on "Keep anyway" to proceed with the download.

Step 5

Once the file is downloaded, open ICMP Installer.exe to install the ICMP Monitoring Tool.

Step 6: Dealing with Windows Defender SmartScreen

If Windows Defender SmartScreen prevents you from running ICMP Installer.exe, follow these steps:

  1. In the SmartScreen pop-up window, click on "More info".
  2. After clicking "More info", a "Run anyway" button will appear.
  3. Click on "Run anyway" to start the installation process.
